Terms & Conditions

1. Services Offered

UdaanBase provides complete architectural design services including:

  • 2D Floor Plans with precise measurements
  • Front & Side Elevation Designs
  • 3D Models with Photorealistic Renders
  • Walkthrough Videos (for selected packages)
  • Structural Drawing Support

2. Pricing & Packages

Package Price Inclusions
Basic 2D Plan ₹2/sq.ft Dimensioned floor plan only
Standard Package ₹4/sq.ft 2D plan + Elevations + Basic 3D views
Premium Package ₹5/sq.ft Complete set with HD renders & walkthrough video

* Final pricing may vary based on project complexity and specific requirements.

3. Payment Terms

  • We accept payments via UPI (PhonePe, GPay, etc.) and Bank Transfer
  • 30% advance payment required to initiate work
  • Remaining 70% to be paid before final delivery of files
  • All payments are non-refundable once work has commenced

4. Delivery Timeline

  • Simple 2D Plans: 1-3 working days
  • 3D Models & Renders: 3-5 working days
  • Complete Packages with Video: 5-7 working days

Timeline begins after receiving all required inputs and advance payment.

5. Revisions Policy

  • 2 minor revisions included in all packages (dimension adjustments, window/door position changes)
  • Major changes requiring redesign may incur additional charges
  • Structural changes after approval may require new payment

6. File Delivery

Upon full payment, you will receive:

  • PDF, JPG, and editable source files (where applicable)
  • MP4 format for walkthrough videos
  • Delivery via Google Drive, Email, or WhatsApp as preferred
